Connect with fellow students, enjoy fun games, raffles, and events!

ElevateCS is a CSU student organization dedicated to bridging the gap between the classroom and the professional world. Our goal is to provide CS students with the tools, confidence, and connections they need to succeed.

ElevateCS provides an environment where students can network, build new skills, and participate in fun and educational activities. We host a variety of events, including workshops, coding challenges, networking events and more. Join us to elevate your journey!
